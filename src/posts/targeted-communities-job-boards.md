I have been compiling up a list of communities where companies can pay to post jobs on their website if they wish to target specific communities, in particular, underrepresented communities in Tech. These websites often will post these job advertisements on their website as well in their newsletters.

* [peopleofcolorintech.com](https://peopleofcolorintech.com/)
* [codingblackfemales.com](https://codingblackfemales.com/)
* [www.diversifytech.co](https://www.diversifytech.co/)
* [blacktechpipeline.com](https://blacktechpipeline.com/)
* [www.womenwhocode.com](https://www.womenwhocode.com/)
* [elpha.com](https://elpha.com/)
* [www.adaslist.co](https://www.adaslist.co/)
* [remotewoman.com](https://remotewoman.com/)
* [www.hireblacknow.com](https://www.hireblacknow.com/)
